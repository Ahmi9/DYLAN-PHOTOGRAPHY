The Storyteller

Behind the lens, a quiet storyteller.

I'm Pratheep — the photographer and visual storyteller behind Dylan Photography. With over a decade of experience capturing weddings across cultures and continents, I've built my craft around one belief: your wedding day is more than a moment. It's a carefully crafted story of love, culture, and connection.

From intimate Nikkah ceremonies to grand South Asian celebrations, I bring a cinematic eye, a calm presence, and a deep respect for every tradition I'm trusted to document.

Based in Toronto. Available worldwide.
